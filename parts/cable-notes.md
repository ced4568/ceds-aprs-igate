# Cable Notes

## Audio crossing rule
- Radio SP out → Sound card MIC in (pink)
- Sound card speaker out (green) → Radio MIC in

## Connector size correction
The QRZ-1 style radio uses different jack sizes:
- SP = larger jack
- MIC = smaller jack

Because of this, a 2.5 mm to 3.5 mm adapter cable is required on the MIC side.

## Recommended quantity
- Buy 2 for production use: one for home, one for mobile
- Buy 3 if you want one spare
